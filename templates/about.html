About Krishi.ai

Krishi.ai is dedicated to transforming rural India through AI-powered solutions, empowering farmers and communities with tools for agriculture, finance, governance, and livestock management.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between technology and rural India, providing accessible, multilingual AI tools that enhance productivity, financial stability, and community resilience.

Our Vision

A thriving rural India where every farmer and community leverages AI to achieve sustainable growth, informed decision-making, and equitable opportunities.

Our Impact

Farmers Supported

Over 10,000 farmers empowered with AI-driven crop planning and disease management tools.

Financial Inclusion

Helped 5,000+ rural households access microloans and government schemes.

Livestock Health

Improved health outcomes for 2,000+ livestock through our veterinary assistant.
